Summary

Hello! My name is Lolita, I'm a graduate from LCC International University Year 2021. I have studied International Business Administration and I really like marketing. For 2 years I was working in university as the dormitories' administrator and for 1 one year as a freelanced translator. While I was studying I have had practicum in a marketing company Global Key.

In 2017 I have finished Adomas Brakas' art school in Klaipeda. My final diploma work in art school was in graphic genre and called "Muzikuojančios lėlės" or in English - "Dolls that play music". After graduation < it was exhibited in Pranas Domšaitis art gallery here in Klaipeda and in other Baltic countries.
